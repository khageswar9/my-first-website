ODISHA

Odisha is an Indian State located in the Eastern region of India. Odisha State is established on 1-Apr-1936. The largest city in Odisha is Bhubaneswar. Total area of Odisha is 1,55,707 km² including 1,52,355.34 km² rural area and 3,351.66 km² urban area. Odisha has a population of 4,19,74,218 peoples. There are 96,37,820 houses in the state. Oriya is the official language of Odisha, which is spoken by the majority of the population. As per ISO 3166-2:IN standard - the ISO code of Odisha is IN-OR.
