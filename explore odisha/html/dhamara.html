Dhamara Port

The Indian state Odisha (Orissa) has a long cost line of 500 km that causing great public interest and excitement to this heavenly land. In this place many fabulous beaches and tremendous sea ports are present those are highly demanding tourist destinations for tourist in this state. One of the great newly born sea port is Dhamara Port. This is a wonderful place for tourists as well as for local people to visit for entrainment. For a refreshment or for fun people love to visit this port of Dhamara and enjoy exploring big ships and winsome surroundings. The coast was protected by a 5 km belt of mangroves, the deep water, nearby Bhitarkanika Mangroves, nearby Gahirmatha Marine Sanctuary, Olive Ridley turtles and many more attractions are available here for visitors.
