Emami Temple

New Jagannath Temple at Balgopalpur in Balasore, district of Odisha, India by Emami Paper Mills. Lord Jagannath is above caste, creed and religion. The temple is 78 feet high scintillating shrine and it has been constructed on a three acres of land on the premises of Balasore town Balgopalpur-based Emami Paper Mill. The temple claimed to world be a replica of the Puri JagannathTemple also bears the world- renowned Black Pagoda Konark Sun Temple's tinge with sixteen Konark Wheels Engraved on it apart from four horses. A beautiful place with amazing architecture and wonderfull senario.
