Chandipur Sea Beach

Charming Chandipur , one of thr finest beaches of India, lies 16kms. from Balasore and is well connected by road. The beach backed up by the swaying casuarinatree and sand-dunes covered by wild creepers experience a unique phenomenon, as the waters recedes here about five kilometers offshore and proceeds rhythmically after a regular interval everyday. One can enjoy literally walking into the sea and also collect abundant sea-shells and driftwood along the beach.
